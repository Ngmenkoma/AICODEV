Who We Are

Our mission, vision, and thematic focus areas guide our commitment to sustainable and inclusive community development in Ghana.

Our Mission

AICODEV Ghana works to change lives and build communities by ensuring equal access to resources, social services, and social justice for all, especially in underserved and rural communities.

Our Vision

A world where everyone enjoys equal rights and equal access to essential services, opportunities, and a dignified quality of life.

Our Thematic Areas

Health

Promoting access to quality healthcare and health education.

Human Rights

Advocating for equality, justice, and protection of human rights.

Poverty & Livelihoods

Empowering communities through sustainable livelihoods.

Education

Supporting inclusive and quality education for all.

Water & Sanitation

Improving access to safe water and sanitation facilities.

Nutrition

Promoting food security and proper nutrition practices.
